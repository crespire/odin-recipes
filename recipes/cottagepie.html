Cottage Pie

Description

A warm, filling serving of pie will make you feel better.

Ingredients

  • Potates
  • Milk and butter
  • Meats
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven
  2. Boil potatoes
  3. Cook meats with seasoning
  4. Mash potatoes
  5. Add cooked meat to baking dish
  6. Top with mashed potato
  7. Bake for 20 minutes
  8. Portion for serving
